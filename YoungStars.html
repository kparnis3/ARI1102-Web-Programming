Floriana Young Stars HC

About & Standing


About

Founded on 27th August 1973 with the president elect being Joe Young.
After that, Young Stars went on to win division II without suffering a single defeat then they proceeded to win their first league division title in 1976. During the 1990's the club introduced Floriana into their name in honor to formalise the special relationship they had together.
